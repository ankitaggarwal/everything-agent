What am I looking at?

This is the whole app. Your mic is streamed up to Gemini continuously (it never waits for a wake word); Gemini decides when you've stopped, thinks, and streams audio back. There are exactly two outcomes from the black box: it calls a tool, or it just talks — and with no tools wired, every turn takes the "just talk" path and ends.

Tip: switch the scenario, press play, and watch the console mirror the real events the app prints (listening, hearing, thinking, speaking…).
